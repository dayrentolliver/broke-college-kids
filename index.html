Feed a Hungry College Student

Help a student in need now!


Feed a Hungry College Student was founded in 2001 by John Smith. As a former hungry, college student Smith knew what it was like to constantly go without food. After graduating, he decided to start this program. The mission of Feed a Hungry College Student is to provide free or more affordable meals to students in need. To date, over 1000 students have benefitted from this program. For just $15 dollars a month, you can help a student in need.
